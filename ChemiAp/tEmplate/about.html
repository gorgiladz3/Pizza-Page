About Us

Welcome to Ananos Pizza, where passion meets taste! Founded with a love for authentic flavors and a commitment to quality, Ananos Pizza has been delighting pizza lovers since 2024. Nestled in the heart of Tbilisi, our pizzeria is more than just a place to eat; it's a gathering spot where families and friends come together to savor delicious, handcrafted pizzas. At Ananos Pizza, we believe in using only the finest ingredients sourced locally and internationally. Our dough is made fresh daily, ensuring each bite is perfectly crisp yet delightfully soft. We take pride in our rich tomato sauce, bursting with flavors that complement our carefully selected toppings. Whether you prefer classic favorites like Margherita and Pepperoni or adventurous specialties like BBQ Chicken and Mediterranean Veggie, there's a pizza to satisfy every palate. Beyond our commitment to exceptional food, Ananos Pizza is dedicated to creating memorable experiences. Our warm and inviting atmosphere welcomes guests to relax and enjoy a slice of happiness. From our friendly staff who are passionate about pizza to our cozy dining area and convenient take-out options, we strive to make every visit special. As a family-owned business, community is at the heart of what we do. We proudly support local events and charities, believing in giving back to the neighborhood that has embraced us. Whether you're joining us for a quick lunch, celebrating a milestone, or hosting a pizza party, Ananos Pizza is here to make every moment deliciously unforgettable. Join us at Ananos Pizza and experience pizza perfection, crafted with love and served with a smile. Discover why we're not just another pizzeria, but your go-to destination for the best pizza in town
